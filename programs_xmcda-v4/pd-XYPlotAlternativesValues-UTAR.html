XYPlotAlternativesValues

Provider:UTAR
Version:1.0

Description

Give a graphical XY representation of two alternativesValues (ex: ranking), providing an efficient way to compare them

  • Contact: Boris Leistedt (boris.leistedt@gmail.com)
